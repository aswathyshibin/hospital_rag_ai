EMERGENCY RESPONSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)

1. PURPOSE
To ensure rapid and effective response during medical emergencies.

2. EMERGENCY TYPES
- Cardiac arrest
- Trauma cases
- Fire emergencies
- Mass casualty incidents

3. CODE BLUE
- Activated during cardiac arrest
- Emergency team must respond within 2 minutes

4. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
- Nurse: Activate emergency code
- Doctor: Lead resuscitation
- Support staff: Manage equipment

5. EMERGENCY EQUIPMENT
- Defibrillator
- Oxygen supply
- Emergency drugs

6. DOCUMENTATION
All emergency interventions must be documented accurately.

7. POST-EMERGENCY REVIEW
A review meeting must be conducted after each emergency event.

8. TRAINING
Mock drills should be conducted quarterly.
