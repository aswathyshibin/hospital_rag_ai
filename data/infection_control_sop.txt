INFECTION CONTROL STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)

1. PURPOSE
To prevent and control hospital-acquired infections and ensure patient and staff safety.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all hospital departments including ICU, wards, OPD, and laboratories.

3. HAND HYGIENE
- Wash hands before and after patient contact
- Use alcohol-based hand rubs when appropriate
- Follow WHO hand hygiene moments

4. PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE)
- Gloves must be worn during patient contact
- Masks are mandatory in sterile areas
- Gowns should be used when exposure is anticipated

5. ICU INFECTION CONTROL
- Strict aseptic techniques must be followed
- ICU equipment must be disinfected daily
- Visitor access should be limited

6. BIOMEDICAL WASTE MANAGEMENT
- Waste must be segregated by color coding
- Sharps must be disposed in puncture-proof containers
- Waste must be removed daily

7. ISOLATION PROTOCOL
- Patients with infectious diseases must be isolated
- Dedicated staff should be assigned

8. CLEANING AND DISINFECTION
- Floors cleaned twice daily
- High-touch surfaces disinfected frequently

9. TRAINING
Staff must receive regular infection control training.

10. AUDIT
Monthly audits must be conducted to ensure compliance.
